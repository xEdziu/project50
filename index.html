PROJECT50

Project50 is a lifestyle challenge made for the top 1% of tomorrow. If you're a creative, athlete, entrepreneur, high-performer or if you just want to start living your life to the fullest this is for you. Ever saw someone and thought to yourself: How do they do all of it? We spent years connecting, learning and studying a group of people that are the most successful, ambitious sought-after in their industry: The so called „Top 1%“. But... who are they? The people that belong to this group are to be found everywhere. They are the ones that outperform 99% of the rest. All the time. Although all of these people are very unique, after analyzing their characters, it was more than clear to us, that they all have certain habits in common, which put them at the top of the chain and make them so successful. We took the 7 most important traits, and formed them into an every-day lifestyle challenge, consisting of 7 rules. We challenge you, to follow these rules for 50 days, the exact time it takes to form habits to keep them forever. If you want to be part of the 1%, if you want to turn your life around and maximize your potential, you need to start this challenge. There is more to the life you're living. Stop depriving yourself of what you can be and start now.

The 7 most important practices included under this challenge are:

  1. RULE 1: WAKE UP BEFORE 8 A.M.

    Every day for 50 days you need to wake up before 8AM. Having said that, you need to get at least 6 hours of sleep and maintain a consistent bed routine. Having a proper sleep cycle is a must. It is proven that waking up early puts an individual in good mood throughout the day. It also gives you more time in the day to complete all your tasks.

  2. RULE 2: DO YOUR MORNING ROUTINE

    Do your morning routine: 1h no distractions (no phone and social media). Set yourself a proper morning routine that you'd like to follow for the next 50 days. Your morning routine can include activities like making your bed, drinking your morning coffee/tea, meditating, spending some time with your loved ones, or spending some time with yourselves to reflect, drinking adequate water, planning your day, your errands, etc. Anything that keeps you away from your distractions will do.

  3. RULE 3: EXERCISE FOR 1H A DAY

    Physical activity is very important. Under this challenge, you need to work out for 1 hour daily, either you can choose to continue performing the same activity every day or you can choose to have any kind of physical activity done. You can choose from- swimming, cycling, working out, yoga, playing any sport, etc. Make sure you do not miss a day. Performing any kind of physical activity every day not only keeps you fit physically but also keeps your mental health in check.

  4. RULE 4: READ 10 PAGES A DAY

    Reading at least 10 pages of your chosen book is the next rule. Reading helps to enhance your vocabulary and knowledge. It also increases your imagination and creativity, giving you a better understanding of how to improve yourself if it's a self-help book or if you're interested in history, it gives you a better understanding in that area. Additionally, reading helps to relax and reduces stress. Moreover, it keeps you away from the distractions that are doing us no good.

  5. RULE 5: DEDICATE 1H TOWARDS A NEW SKILL

    I've realized that due to our hectic schedule we neglect the skills we're keen to learn about. For example, ever since I can remember I want to improve my programming skills but because my day is packed with mundane tasks, I do not make any extra efforts to take out time and learn it. Under project 50 you have to diligently give 1 hour to the skill you really want to pursue. Learning a new skill, without any doubt, adds to your knowledge and keeps you up to date. In addition, it also helps you to relax when you do something you absolutely love. It can be singing, painting, coding, designing, anything that you want to master.

  6. RULE 6: FOLLOW A HEALTHY DIET

    We are so concerned about how we look, what we wear, etc. but we seem to be neglecting what we put into our body which eventually gets us healthy-looking skin, healthier hair and so much more. Having a healthy diet benefits us in areas such as it keeps us fit, giving us healthy, glowing skin, it also improves our mental health. It is recommended to have no junk food for these 50 days. No cheat days. No junk food.

  7. RULE 7: TRACK YOUR DAILY PROGRESS IN A JOURNAL

    Keeping a journal helps to track your progress. It is a proven fact that keeping a journal helps in gaining confidence as well. After 50 days when you look back and go through our progress, you'll be nothing but proud of yourselves.

  8. GENERAL PROJECT50 RULE

    If you fail on any of the 50 days, start over at day 1. Repeat until finished.
